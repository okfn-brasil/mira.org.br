English
The Map of Open Resource Iniciatives (MIRA) project ended in 2015.
We no longer update the site, but make it available on a new address: mira.educacaoaberta.org.
If you have questions or comments please get in touch through this form.
We invite you to check to a project we now contribute to - the OER World Map.
